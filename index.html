How It Works

From invite to hire-ready in 4 steps

Your part takes 2 minutes. The system handles the rest.

1

Invite Driver

Send a magic link via SMS. Driver opens it on their phone — no app download needed.

2

Scan & Apply

The driver takes a photo of their CDL with their phone camera. The system reads it and fills in their application automatically.

3

Auto-Verify

The system pulls driving records, checks the FMCSA Clearinghouse, and orders the pre-employment drug test — all without you lifting a finger.

4

Hire Ready

Once everything clears, your driver's complete qualification file is assembled and audit-ready from day one. No paperwork left behind.
