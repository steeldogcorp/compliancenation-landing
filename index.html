How It Works

From invite to hire-ready in 4 steps

What used to take weeks of paperwork now happens in minutes.

1

Invite Driver

Send a magic link via SMS. Driver opens it on their phone — no app download needed.

2

Scan & Apply

Driver scans their CDL and medical card with their camera. AI extracts data and auto-fills the FMCSA application.

3

Auto-Verify

The system runs MVR checks, PSP lookups, Clearinghouse queries, and drug test orders automatically.

4

Hire Ready

Complete DQF assembled. Compliance score calculated. Driver is cleared and audit-ready from day one.
