Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Compliance Nation ("we," "us," or "our") operates the compliancenation.com website and the Compliance Nation platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Carrier Account Information — Company name, DOT number, MC number, contact name, email address, phone number, and billing information
  • Driver Information — Name, date of birth, Social Security number, CDL number and state, medical card details, phone number, email address, employment history, and address
  • Compliance Records — Drug and alcohol testing results, driving record (MVR) data, FMCSA Clearinghouse query results, and driver qualification file documents
  • Usage Data — Log data, device information, IP addresses, and interactions with our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our compliance management services
  • Build and manage driver qualification files
  • Administer DOT drug and alcohol testing programs
  • Send compliance notifications via SMS and email (with your consent)
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Comply with FMCSA regulations and DOT requirements
  • Improve our platform and customer experience

3. SMS Communications

Drivers who opt in during onboarding consent to receive SMS messages related to DOT compliance notifications, including random drug and alcohol testing alerts and account notifications. SMS consent is collected directly from the driver and is not shared with third parties or used for marketing purposes. For full details, see our Driver SMS Consent page.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Your Motor Carrier — Driver information is shared with the carrier that enrolled the driver in the program
  • Testing Laboratories & MROs — As required to administer drug and alcohol testing programs
  • FMCSA Clearinghouse — As required by federal regulation for query and reporting
  • Service Providers — Payment processors, cloud hosting, and communications services that assist in operating our platform
  • Legal Requirements — When required by law, regulation, or legal process

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and audit logging. Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as Social Security numbers, dates of birth, and license numbers are masked by default in our interfaces and require authorized access to view.

6. Data Retention

Compliance records are retained in accordance with FMCSA record retention requirements (49 CFR Part 382 and Part 391). Carrier and driver account data is retained for the duration of the service relationship and the legally required retention period thereafter. Compliance events and attestation records are maintained as immutable audit records.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. Please note that certain compliance records cannot be deleted due to federal regulatory requirements. Drivers may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised effective date.
